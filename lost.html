Lostwave

What is Lostwave?

Lostwave is a term used to describe obscure media from an unknown source, with information, including title, artist, album, and dates, being generally unknown or scarce

Examples of Lostwave

  • The Most Mysterious Song on the Internet
  • Everyone Knows That
  • On The Roof
  • D>E>A>T>H>M>E>T>A>L>
  • Ready 'n' Steady

The boombox commonly associated with the popular lostwave song, Everyone Knows That (or EKT)
